Saint Joseph Bangna

Mare Anna de Jesus, Dean of St. Paul de Chartres wishes to build St. Joseph Bangna School for
female students in Samut Prakan province and in the metropolitan area to receive quality education.

St. Joseph Bangna School is one of the school in the St. Paul de Chartre group, was authorized
to open teaching on May 21, 1968 with the first generation of 650 students. The school are being classified
as a large general elementary school and medium secondary schools
The school implementing the teaching and learning program according to
the Ministry of Education curriculum in English (English Program) since the academic year of 2004.

The Congregation of the Sisters of St. Paul of Chartres (SPC) is a Roman Catholic religious apostolic
missionary congregation of pontifical right for teaching, nursing, visiting the poor and taking care
of orphans, the old and infirm, and the mentally ill. It was founded in Levesville-la-Chenard, France in 1696.
